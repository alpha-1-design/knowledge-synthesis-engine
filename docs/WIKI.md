# Semantic Mesh Wiki

Welcome to the foundational philosophy of the Knowledge Synthesis Engine.

## What is a "Research Void"?
In scientific literature, a "Research Void" occurs when two fields (e.g., Solid State Physics and Neural Architecture) have structural isomorphisms (similar patterns) but no direct citations. KSE is designed to hunt these voids.

## The Concept of "Grounded Persistence"
Most AI tools are "Transient"—they provide an answer and forget. KSE is "Persistent." Every discovery is a permanent addition to the Knowledge Mesh, building a cumulative research asset for the institution.

## Multi-Agent "Logical Checks"
We believe in a "Separation of Concerns" for AI:
*   **The Hunter (Scout):** Optimized for broad search.
*   **The Architect (Extractor):** Optimized for schema and data structure.
*   **The Critic (Auditor):** Optimized for logic and rigor.

## Glossary of Terms
*   **CID:** Content-Addressable Identifier.
*   **Topological Mesh:** A graph where the "shape" of connections represents the health of a research field.
*   **Synthesis Opportunity:** A potential breakthrough suggested by cross-domain mapping.
