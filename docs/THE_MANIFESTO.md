# THE MANIFESTO: The Soul of the Semantic Mesh

This document defines the core philosophy, the "harness," and the "seasons" of the Knowledge Synthesis Engine.

## 1. The Central Harness: Truth through Anchoring
The KSE revolves around a single constraint: **The Semantic Mesh Protocol.** 
In this system, truth is not "generated"—it is "anchored." An AI can propose a connection, but the Protocol demands that the connection be tethered to a verifiable CID (Content-Addressable Identifier). 

## 2. The Four Pillars of the Mesh
1.  **Grounded Persistence:** Knowledge is an asset. It must be stored, versioned, and protected against loss.
2.  **Agent Sovereignty:** We employ a "Separation of Concerns." No single LLM has total authority. Discovery, Extraction, and Auditing are handled by independent intelligence agents.
3.  **Topological Integrity:** We believe knowledge has a "shape." By visualizing research in 3D space, we allow the human mind to perform the final, most critical layer of pattern recognition.
4.  **Cryptographic Provenance:** Every node in the graph is traceable. We do not accept "black box" answers.

## 3. The Five Seasons of the Research Cycle
Knowledge in the KSE is not static; it flows through five distinct seasons:
*   **Hunger:** Identifying the "Research Voids" in the current mesh.
*   **Harvest:** The ingestion of raw scientific literature from global repositories.
*   **Sifting:** The extraction of atomic concepts and relationships from dense text.
*   **Marriage:** The synthesis of cross-domain hypotheses (The "Synthesis Opportunity").
*   **Judgment:** The critical logical audit that validates or rejects the new synthesis.

*The Semantic Mesh is not just a database; it is a protocol for the future of human discovery.*
